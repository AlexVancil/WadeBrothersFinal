About the Wade Brothers

Founded back in 2013, Wade Brothers has been St. Louis owned and operated by three creative brothers that have worked hand-in-hand in numerous projects. Now, they want to help you! Getting St. Louis talent recognized has always been a pillar of Wade Brothers, and they've been consistently helping people reach their full potential.

Dylan Wade

Motivated and crafty, Dylan is the oldest Wade brother and has had a passion for guitars his entire life. Graduating from Roberto-Venn School of Luthierie, he has many years of hands-on experinece both repairing and creating guitars.

Andrew Wade

Audio has always been an interest of Andrew, the middle Wade brother. Being in the voice acting field and taking part in several large productions has given Andrew professional insight into how to make things sound Hollywood quality.

Jayden Wade

The youngest of the Wade brothers, and the most technically proficient. Jayden has worked as a mechanical engineer and uses his knowledge of technology to help make your recordings sound seamless and the best it can possibly be.
